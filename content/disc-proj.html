Discipline Projects

3D Graphics Systems

This course is an introductory graphics course emphasizing the practical aspects of the discipline. It covers the fundamentals of modeling and rendering of surfaces in three dimensions. These topics are presented in the context of a computer graphics system.

Course page:

  • http://lvelho.impa.br/i3d18/

Projects:

  • Procedural generation of planar tree-like structures with Blender: Root Of Shame
  • Procedural generation and growth animation of 3D trees with Unity: Phatom Trees

Fundamentals and Trends in Vision and Image Processing

This course is an introductory image processing course and trends. It covers the fundamentals of image processing and vision together with the trending topic of artificial neural networks.

Course page:

  • http://lvelho.impa.br/ip18/

Projects:

  • Image to image translation using generative models: CycleGAN: Photo to Drawing

Reading Course: Machine learning applied to computer media

This course was an introductory reinforcement learning course. It covered the fundamentals of reinforcement learning and the trending topic of deep reinforcement learning.

Projects:
